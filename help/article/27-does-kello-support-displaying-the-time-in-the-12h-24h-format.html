Does Kello support displaying the time in the 12h or 24h format?

Yes. Kello uses the same time format than the one on the phone it's been paired with.

If you want to display the time in another format, go to your phone's date and time settings, change the format, kill the Kello app and launch it again. Kello will update its time format.
